Soft Skills

Interpersonal Skills

  • Team Collaboration: Thrive in collaborative environments, contributing ideas, supporting peers, and achieving shared goals efficiently.
  • Effective Communication: Convey technical and non-technical information clearly through reports, presentations, and discussions.
  • Empathy & Leadership: Understand colleagues’ perspectives, foster strong working relationships, and guide teams toward success.
  • Conflict Resolution: Manage disagreements constructively, facilitating solutions that maintain productivity and harmony.

Intrapersonal Skills

  • Time Management: Prioritize tasks effectively to meet deadlines and maintain high-quality work standards.
  • Self-Motivation: Take initiative in learning new tools, tackling challenges, and completing projects independently.
  • Adaptability: Adjust to evolving project requirements, new technologies, and dynamic work environments.
  • Critical Thinking: Analyze problems logically, evaluate solutions, and make informed, data-driven decisions.

Technical Skills

  • Python, SQL, R: Perform data cleaning, statistical analysis, automation, and predictive modeling on diverse datasets.
  • Power BI, Tableau, Excel: Design interactive dashboards, visualize trends, and present actionable insights for decision-making.
  • Web Development (HTML, CSS, JavaScript): Build responsive, user-friendly websites and applications with modern web standards.
  • Git & GitHub: Manage version control, collaborate on code, and deploy projects efficiently to live platforms.
