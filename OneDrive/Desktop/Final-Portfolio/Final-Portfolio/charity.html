Giving Back to the Community

I actively participate in local community development initiatives, with a strong focus on digital literacy and education support. These include facilitating digital skills workshops and participating in school donation drives.

Recently, I initiated a school data management system aimed at helping rural schools efficiently manage student records, track academic performance, and improve data-driven decision-making at the administrative level.

Through these efforts, I aim to use technology and data to create meaningful, sustainable impact within underserved communities.
